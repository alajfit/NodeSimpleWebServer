Your objective is to fly your ship through the city limits crate fields.

Gameplay

This game will test your Sanity to its limits. The aim of this game is to get the spacecraft through as many stacked crates as possible. These crates are situated on the outer limits of some very well known cities. There are medals and achievements for getting past a certain amount of crates in the game.

Controls

To move the ship up you must tap the screen. There are also multiple buttons available for different modes.

Music

The Music is provided by a Royalty free Website.
